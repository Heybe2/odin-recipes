Tallaabooyinka Samaynta Lasagna

  1. Diyaari maaddooyinka oo dhan.
  2. Ku shiil hilibka lo'da saliid saytuun ah iyo basasha ilaa ay noqoto mid bunni ah.
  3. Kudar toonta, yaanyada shiidan, iyo xawaashka kadibna kari ilaa 15 daqiiqo.
  4. Diyaari maraqa bechamel: kululee caanaha, kudar burka iyo farmaajada, walaaq si uu u adkaado.
  5. U diyaari saxanka foornada, dhig lakab lasagna ah, kadib hilib, kadib maraqa bechamel, kuna celceli.
  6. Ku shub farmaajo badan dusha sare.
  7. Ku rid foornada kulul 180°C kuna kari 40-45 daqiiqo.
  8. Ka saar foornada kadibna ha nasto 10 daqiiqo ka hor intaadan jarin.
  9. U diyaari saxan oo u adeegso kulul.
